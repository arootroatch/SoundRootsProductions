Who We Are

Sound Roots Productions is a live event production company specializing in Nashville's bar scene and small clubs. We provide your venue with a team of skilled audio and lighting professionals to handle all the technical aspects of your event production and system maintenance. No more trying to hire your own sound engineers or limiting your imagination because of productions shortcomings. You dream up the event, we handle the rest.

Our Philosophy

We believe that event production is, at its core, a customer service industry. An audio engineer must serve the audience, venue staff, and musicians on stage simultaneously in a fast-paced environment. For the Sound Roots team, the "salty sound guy" stereotype does not apply. Everyone on our team is a passionate engineer who loves what they do and who they work with.
