Who We Are

Sound Roots Productions is a live event production company specializing in Nashville's bar scene and small clubs. We provide your venue with a Production Manager and a team of skilled audio and lighting professionals to handle all the technical aspects of your event production and system maintenance. No more trying to hire your own sound engineers or limiting your imagination because of productions shortcomings. You dream up the event, we handle the rest.

Powered by Curator.io

Our Philosophy

We believe that event production is, at its core, a customer service industry. An audio engineer must serve the audience, venue staff, and musicians on stage simultaneously in a fast-paced environment. For the Sound Roots team, the "salty sound guy" stereotype does not apply. Everyone on our team is a passionate engineer who loves what they do and who they work with. We will make sure to create a memorable experience for the audience, and a seamless experience for musicians and venue staff that rivals the venues next door.

Audio

We have a whole team of experienced audio engineers from a variety of backgrounds. We've done everything from recording studio sessions to arena shows, writers rounds to festivals, and full-band showcases to multi-track live recordings. No matter what your event is, we can handle it in stride.

DMX Light
Programming

Are you looking to add to the vibe of your space or make your stage lighting pop? From install to programming, we have you covered. We can help you pick the best lights for your needs and budget, and our lighting designers can program a scene for any occasion.

Maintenance, Repair,
and Minor Install

Nobody likes it when equipment doesn't work properly, especially when fixing it seems overwhelming or tedious. That's where Sound Roots Productions comes in. We can take care of the repair process for any lights or speakers that need attention and check that your system is setup to minimize equipment damage. If you have gear that has transformed into paper weights, Sound Roots is happy to put it back to work for you!

System Control
and Programming

Install companies do a great job, but systems often need extra customization to fit your specific needs. We can customize and reconfigure your system to add functionality that the install company couldn't foresee. Additionally, with compatible equipment, we can design custom iPad apps to make controlling your audio and lighting systems easy.
